Your gateway to Grin.

Grin↗ is a cryptocurrency focused on privacy and scalability, built using some of the most recent advancements in blockchain and cryptography research.

It supports transactions with a high degree of privacy, and atomic swaps that eliminate the need for centralised exchanges. But the interactive nature of the protocol make these difficult to carry out in practice.

Our open source product suite allows you to store, swap, and send Grin seamlessly through a single interface.

  • Get Started

Transacting in Grin has many practical challenges.

  • Interactivity

    Transactions require senders and receivers to exchange information back and forth and manage the state of these messages.

  • Security

    Grin is not supported by many exchanges, and forces you to entrust your funds and personal information with these third parties.

  • Privacy

    Transaction aggregation, used to hide hide your transactions amongst others, is difficult to achieve using the default propagation method.

vault713 is the perfect companion to Grin.

  • Transaction building

    Seamless routing of messages that does not require you to be online and does not put your funds at risk.

  • Cross-chain Swapping

    Complete atomic swaps directly from your wallet, making it easy to trade in and out of Grin.

  • Transaction Aggregation

    Your transactions are joined with those of other users before being broadcasted, making tracing more difficult.

The vault713 suite

  • wallet713 is the main interface to our services. Store grin safely, manage your balances, and access other Vault713 services.
  • 713.grinswap provides trustless P2P atomic swaps between Grin and Bitcoin. Trade without entrusting others with your personal details or funds.
  • 713.grinbox is a transaction relay that facilitates Grin transactions and protects your identity, without compromising the security of your funds.
