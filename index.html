We make it easy to store, send, and swap Grin.

Grin↗ is a cryptocurrency focused on privacy and scalability, promising censorship resistant electronic transactions. Vault713 builds open source solutions that make it easy for users and businesses alike to get up and running with Grin and make the most out of the technology.

Get started

Transact easily through relays

Send funds to unique addresses that are not published on-chain, and do not require transacting parties to be online at the same time.

Buy & Sell using Atomic Swaps

Trade Grin without the relying on centralised exchanges, and without entrusting third parties with your funds and personal information. (Coming soon)

Stay private with aggregation

Mix your transactions with those of other users before they are broadcasted to the blockchain, to make tracing more difficult. (Coming soon)

Developer & Enterprise solutions

713.grinswap

Host your own federated transaction relay that facilitates Grin transaction building. Keep your data protected and remain fully independent.
